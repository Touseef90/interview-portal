1. What do you think of AI?
I use AI tools like ChatGPT or Claude extensively as a productivity multiplier. I'll prompt the AI to brainstorm potential edge cases or point out things I might have missed. But I always analyze the suggestions critically to ensure I fully understand the implementation before integrating it. I use AI to assist your workflow, not to generate entire features blindly. I think AI is expansion of hands not expansion of brains. I believe if you don't know the basics of coding even AI can't help you.
